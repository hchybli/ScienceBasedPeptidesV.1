# How to read a certificate of analysis (COA)

A COA summarizes batch testing for identity, purity, and sometimes residual solvent classes depending on the panel selected by the laboratory.

## Typical sections

- **Identification**: confirms the intended primary sequence or molecular signature.
- **Purity**: chromatographic purity as a percentage with method noted.
- **Appearance**: physical description of the received material.
- **Storage**: recommendations for lyophilized stability windows.

## Batch matching

Always verify the batch number printed on the label against the COA filename and report body.
